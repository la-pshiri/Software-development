Our services

Indoor navigation app development

We as the Campus cab team develop customized indoor navigation applications for businesses, universities and colleges. The aim for our applications is to provide users with precise directions, real time updates and additional features such as search, promotion and events.

Indoor navigation system integration

The Campus cab team provide indoor navigation system integration services to their clients (businesses, universities and colleges) wishing to enchance their visitors indoor navigation experience. Our system can be integrated into existing applications to provide users with accurate and reliable directions.

Indoor navigation consulting

Consulting services are offered by the Campus cab team for our clients (businesses, universities and colleges) who see the need to improve their indoor navigation experience. Our team of experts can assist with identifying areas for improvement, provide helpful recommendations and assist clients implement solutions.
